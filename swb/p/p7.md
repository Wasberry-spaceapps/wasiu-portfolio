# Brand Voice Style Guide: [Brand Name] Wellness
**Core Identity:** The Grounded Guide. We are the bridge between ancestral wisdom and modern daily life.

---

## 1. Tone & Personality
*   **Grounded:** We use sensory language (soil, scent, roots). We are not "woo-woo" without substance; we are rooted in the earth.
*   **Warm but Authoritative:** We speak like a wise elder or a mentor. We don't use hype or "crush it" culture.
*   **Rhythmic:** Our prose has a natural cadence. Long, descriptive sentences followed by short, impactful ones.

---

## 2. Vocabulary List

| Use These (On-Brand) | Avoid These (Off-Brand) |
| :--- | :--- |
| Ritual, Practice, Rhythm | Routine, Hack, Optimization |
| Botanical, Tincture, Infusion | Chemical, Product, Supplement |
| Ancestral, Time-honored | Trending, Disruption, New-age |
| Nourish, Support, Tend | Fix, Cure, Boost |
| Wild-crafted, Sustainable | Mass-produced, Cheap, Synthesized |

---

## 3. Content Examples

### Website (About Page)
*   **Yes:** "We believe wellness isn't a destination to reach, but a rhythm to return to. Our apothecary is stocked by the seasons, not the markets."
*   **No:** "We offer the best-selling health supplements at the lowest prices to optimize your daily performance."

### Email (Newsletter Introduction)
*   **Yes:** "As the frost settles into the soil this morning, we find ourselves turning toward the warming properties of Ginger and Ceylon Cinnamon..."
*   **No:** "FLASH SALE! Don't miss out on our winter energy boosters. Buy now before they're gone!"
