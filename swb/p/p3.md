# Online Course Customer Onboarding Journey Map
**Project:** Earth-Medicine Practitioner Certification
**Goal:** Increase course completion rates and reduce churn through proactive engagement.

---

## 1. Journey Overview
| Phase | Stage | Primary Touchpoint | Objective |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **01** | Enrollment | Immediate Email / Login Page | Instant access & friction reduction. |
| **02** | The Welcome | Video Intro / Community Invite | Foster belonging and set expectations. |
| **03** | The First Win | Module 1 Quick-Start | Achieve a small, tangible result in <24 hours. |
| **04** | Sustained Momentum | Weekly Progress Emails | Prevent the "Week 3 Slump." |
| **05** | Milestone Check-in | 50% Completion Survey | Address technical or conceptual hurdles. |
| **06** | Graduation | Certificate / Post-Course Path | Transition from student to brand advocate. |

---

## 2. Email Sequence & Milestone Communications

### Email 1: The Welcome (Instant)
- **Subject:** Welcome to the Circle: Your [Course Name] Journey Begins
- **Content:** Login credentials, a 2-minute "Start Here" video, and a prompt to join the private community.
- **CTA:** "Log in to your dashboard."

### Email 2: The First Seed (Day 2)
- **Subject:** A small ritual for your first module
- **Content:** Encouragement to complete the first 10-minute lesson. Includes a downloadable "Study Ritual" PDF (setting intentions).
- **CTA:** "Watch Lesson 1.1."

### Email 3: Community Connection (Day 5)
- **Subject:** Meet your fellow practitioners
- **Content:** Highlighting a thread in the community forum.
- **CTA:** "Introduce yourself here."

### Email 4: The Momentum Boost (Weekly - Monday)

- **Content:** Summary of what they will learn this week + a "Pro-Tip" from the instructor.
- **CTA:** "Continue your studies."

## 3. Milestone Triggered Notifications
*   **Module Completion:** In-app confetti animation + email: "You've unlocked the secrets of [Topic]! Here is a bonus recipe for your apothecary."
*   **Stalled Progress (7 Days Inactive):** Subject: "Is everything okay, [Name]?" (Empathy-driven check-in offering support).
*   **Completion:** Invitation to the Alumni Directory and a discount code for advanced certifications.
